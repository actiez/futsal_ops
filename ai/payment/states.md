# Payment States

## Status List

- unpaid
- proof_submitted
- paid
- rejected
- waived
- cancelled

## Definitions

unpaid:
Payment is expected but not yet submitted

proof_submitted:
Player has uploaded payment proof, pending review

paid:
Payment verified and confirmed by admin

rejected:
Submitted proof is invalid or incorrect

waived:
Payment is excused by admin

cancelled:
Payment is no longer required due to event cancellation
