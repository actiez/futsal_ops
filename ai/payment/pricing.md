# Pricing Model

## Modes

### Individual Pricing
- fixed amount per player

### Lump Sum Pricing
- total amount divided by number of players

## Formula

amount_per_player = total_amount / total_players

## Rounding Rule

All amounts must be rounded UP to nearest cent.

Example:
$200 / 11 = $18.1818 → $18.19

## Surplus Handling

Any surplus from rounding:
- retained by organiser
- not redistributed
